Requirements

  • A general knowledge of Galaxy
  • Knowledge and comfort with the Unix/Linux command line interface (tool integration)
  • Comfort with a text editor (tool integration and training development)
  • An interest in using Galaxy to teach/train people (training development)
Working environment: A normal Linux/Unix environment with a terminal will be used during the training sessions and hackathon.\n\nFor Windows users, we will provide a virtual machine.\n\n

Practical information

Venue

The event will take place at the Biological station of Roscoff. Founded in 1872, the Biological station is presently one of the leading marine laboratories in Europe. It is affiliated with the National Center for Scientific Research (CNRS) and Sorbonne Université.\n\nThe meeting will happen in Hôtel de France, Room 3 (red point in the map). The lunch and accomodation will be at Gulf Stream, the restaurant and hotel (green point in the map) of the Station.\n\nSome restaurants are also added to the map for the dinners.\n\n

Cost

The workshop participation is free of charge. The ELIXIR Galaxy Community and the IFB/ELIXIR-FR will cover the meeting costs (lunch and dinner)\n\n

Accomodation

Bed and breakfast type of accommodation has been pre-booked with the Gulf Stream (green point in the map), the Station Biologique de Roscoff Hotel. You will be accommodated in a single room with shower and toilet.\n\nTravel expenses and lodging need to be covered by each participant individually.\n\n

Travel

Roscoff is a small town located on the northeast coast of Brittany: 16 miles from Morlaix, 37 miles from Brest, 131 miles from Rennes.\n\nRoscoff can be reached\n\n
  • by ferry from Plymouth (United Kingdom),
  • by plane from Paris to Brest, then by taxi,
  • by TGV (high speed train) from Paris (Montparnasse train station or Charles-de-Gaulle airport) to Morlaix, then by local train, bus or taxi.
To share car from the train station or the airport, please register there\n\n
